Our Services

At PT-EXPERT, we offer tailored solutions for post-tensioned slab systems, including design, assessment, strengthening, and sustainability evaluation. Our expertise ensures efficient, durable, and innovative results for every project.

Preliminary PT Slab Design

Start your construction project on the right foot with our preliminary post-tensioned slab design services. Our team of experts conducts thorough analyses to create innovative and sustainable slab designs tailored to your specific requirements. By utilizing advanced engineering techniques, we ensure that your project not only meets safety standards but also maximizes cost-efficiency and constructability.

Detailed Design and Drawings

Transform concepts into construction-ready documentation with our detailed design and drawing services. We deliver precise reinforcement layouts, PT profiles, tendon layouts, and shop drawings that integrate smoothly with your construction workflow.

Assessment of Existing Slabs

Ensure the safety and longevity of existing structures by leveraging our assessment and strengthening expertise. We provide structural evaluation, non-destructive testing recommendations, retrofit design, and strengthening schemes that are practical and cost-effective.

Value Engineering for PT Slabs

Optimize costs without compromising structural integrity. Our value engineering approach helps you select efficient designs, materials, and construction sequences that reduce lifecycle costs and speed up delivery.
